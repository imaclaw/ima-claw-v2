EP01: Adopt Your First Lobster in 10 Minutes

✍ Yuki & Claw

From zero to chatting AI assistant in 10 minutes. No coding required.

Welcome to Ima Claw Academy. This is EP01: How to adopt your first AI lobster in 10 minutes.

1

Get the installer

Mac / Linux:

curl -fsSL https://openclaw.ai/install.sh | bash

Windows:

iwr -useb https://openclaw.ai/install.ps1 | iex
2

Launch the app

Open Terminal (Mac) or Command Prompt / PowerShell (Windows).

Type openclaw and press Enter.

You will see the OpenClaw logo and a setup wizard.

3

Connect to your channels

Choose where you want to chat with your lobster:

  • WhatsApp - scan QR code
  • Feishu / Lark - add the app to your workspace
  • Telegram - create bot via BotFather
  • Discord - create app and get token

Follow the prompts for your chosen channel.

4

Adopt your lobster

Give your assistant a name. Pick an emoji. Set a wake word if you want.

Congratulations! Your first AI employee is ready to work.
